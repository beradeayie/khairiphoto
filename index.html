DSLR FOR

BEGINNER

AUGUST 8 , 2017

Well, this entry will give you a little guide for beginnner / newbie to buy the first DSLR. Before we choose the DSLR model, let's first see what features you need to get the first time to buy the first camera.

1. . Easy to use

 

The interface is attractive, easy and non-fiber.

 

2. Live View

 

Live View is not a mandatory function, but from my experience, LV is required in certain circumstances, especially when it comes to shooting at difficult corners.

 

3 . Cheap Price

 

Typically for the DSLR entry level, you can earn around RM2500. I do not encourage you to buy expensive cameras. It is best to invest in accesories like lenses or flash.

 

4. Your partnet camera

 

Choose the brand that most of my members are using. For example, if many members use Canon, they buy Canon. So it's easy for you to learn or share accessories.

 

Between the DSLR I recommend for the beginner is as follows. Price below rm2000 :

 

 

 

1 . Canon 1100d/1000

2 . Nikon D5100/3200

After purchasing the camera, do not forget to read the manual. DSLR is not a kind of washing machine, no manual reading can be used. So please read your manual . Thanks  ^_^
